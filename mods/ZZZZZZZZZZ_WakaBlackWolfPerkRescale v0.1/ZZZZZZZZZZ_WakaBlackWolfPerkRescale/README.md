# Waka Black Wolf Perk Rescale v0.1

Late-loading companion patch for Black Wolf's better vanilla perks.

## Scope

- Spreads `perkPenetrator` armor bypass across 10 ranks, with full armor ignore only at rank 10.
- Moderates `perkDemolitionsExpert` explosive damage, reload, handling, and dismember scaling.
- Reduces the early `perkBoomstick` point-blank damage spike.
- Pushes Strength Mastery bleed hooks later so rank 1 is not an immediate bleed package.
- Rescales Black Wolf's Flurry, Grand Slam, Hard Target, and Mastery combat spikes so armor bypass, instant-kill, heavy bleed, evasion, and extreme damage multipliers sit later and lower.
- Leaves Better Barter and Daring Adventurer to `ZZZZZZZZ_WakaEconomyPacingPatch`.

## Load Order

The folder name intentionally starts with ten `Z` characters so this patch loads after Black Wolf and existing Waka pacing patches.
